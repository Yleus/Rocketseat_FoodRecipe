PASTA WITH BOLOGNESE SAUCE

Ingredients

  • 1/2 package of pasta (8.81oz penne, screw or tie, preferably wholegrain or durum.
  • 7.05oz to 8.81oz of textured soy protein
  • 1 scoop of water (1.3 cup)
  • 1 tablespoon chopped or grated onion
  • 1 tablespoon minced or crushed garlic
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of oregano
  • 1 tablespoon of parsley
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil

Preparation

  1. Put everything in the electric pressure cooker.
  2. Mix well. Cover the pan. Set the pot valve to the pressure position. Schedule 10 minutes.
  3. When the time is up, remove the pressure from the pan.
  4. Open the lid.
  5. Serve yourself.

Extra information

You can put grated cheese on top. Add olives, basil and whatever else you like.
